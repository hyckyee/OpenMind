How we started the Open Mind project.


Why we started the project:

The unconscious discrimination in our society

Our society has unconscious discrimination. For example, almost every country has cultures that discriminate between males and females, and these cultures appear so frequently that many people unconsciously think that they should discriminate between males and females. This is true for substance minorities. And this is why we started this project.

Why does the filter bubble effect appear?

Then we thought. Why does the modern SNS has filter bubble effect? This is because of their recommendation algorithms. Modern SNS trys to make users stay in their own services. So, they use recommendation algorithms and make users interested. However, that made a huge backfire. Which is called the filter bubble effect.

We found an answer

We tried to get rid of the filter bubble effect. And, it is really a hard job. We asked ourselves tons of questions. Is this solution right? Finally, we found a solution. If we can't get rid of discriminating pieces of information, then we should be exposed to balanced and unbiased pieces of information more!
